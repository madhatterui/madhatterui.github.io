normal priority

  1. Pre-cast Windfury Totem as close as possible to enemies before the pull
  2. Cast Primordial Wave on the way into melee.
  3. Cast Flame Shock on the way into melee.
  4. Cast Flame Shock in AoE on an unaffected target
  5. Cast Feral Spirit
  6. Cast Lava Lash to apply Lashing Flames and spread Flame Shock
  7. Cast Primordial Wave (in AoE: ideally to an enemy not affected by Flame Shock)
  8. Cast Elemental Blast in single target with your first 10 Maelstrom Weapon
  9. Cast Lightning Bolt once you reach 8-10 Maelstrom to spend Primordial Wave
  10. Cast Crash Lightning in AoE to get the buff active
  11. Cast Ice Strike
  12. Cast Fire Nova in AoE
  13. Cast Frost Shock to spend Hailstorm
  14. Cast Lava Lash
  15. Cast Stormstrike if nothing else is ready
  16. Cast Chain Lightning at 5+ Maelstrom to consume Crackling Thunder
  17. Cast Lava Burst as soon as you reach 5+ Maelstrom Weapon stacks
  18. Proceed onto the normal priority list
